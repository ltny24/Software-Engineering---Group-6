# Introduction
*Performed by: Hồ Thị Như Ngọc | Reviewed by: Dương Minh Huỳnh Khôi | Edited by: Dương Minh Huỳnh Khôi, Hồ Thị Như Ngọc*


In a university environment, an integrated academic portal is essential for streamlining operations and maintaining strong connectivity between the institution and its learners. To meet this demand, MyUS is developed as an AI-powered academic portal that fully digitalizes daily administrative and academic activities, establishing a unified workspace for students, faculty, and administrators. The platform seamlessly handles comprehensive operations, from tracking grades and calculating GPA to managing teaching schedules, processing document approvals, and resolving academic complaints online.

MyUS bridges administrative efficiency with an enhanced, data-driven digital learning environment by directly optimizing course registration through smart solutions. By integrating artificial intelligence, the system automatically recommends optimal class schedules tailored to individual preferences and utilizes demand forecasting to prevent classroom overcrowding. Furthermore, MyUS features personalized wishlists for course planning and offers AI-driven support to automatically draft compliant, formal academic requests. Ultimately, the platform eliminates cumbersome paperwork, saves valuable time for all parties involved, and ensures absolute accuracy and security for educational data management.
